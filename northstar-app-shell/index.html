Option B — brand guidelines (the Wayfinder Labs variation, written down)

A platform variation, not a re-statement of the marketing brand: same DNA (ink, blue, green, facets, Arimo), tuned for eight-hour data sessions — quieter surfaces, higher-contrast text, color reserved for meaning.

Color roles
#061D52Wayfinder InkMasthead, footer, brand moments only. Never body text on white.
#0B1B3DText inkBody text. Derived from Wayfinder Ink; 14.9:1 on white.
#2349A1Wayfinder BlueLinks, informational badges, selected states. 7.4:1 on white.
#15803DAction GreenPrimary buttons, success text. The darker cousin of brand green — 4.8:1 on white, AA at any size.
#16A34ABrand GreenAccents, highlight strokes, positive fills. Decorative — not text on white (3.3:1).
#7DF090 #84A1EBFacet tintsThe logo's light facets. Hairline accents on Ink surfaces (active-nav underline, facet bar) only. Never fills, never text.
#A16207AttentionDeadlines, warnings, degraded states. The only non-brand hue — urgency must not be green or blue.
#F5F7FBCanvasPage background. Cards are white with #DBE2EF hairlines.
Type & texture
DisplayArimo 600–700 — masthead wordmark, page titles. Matches the wordmark's grotesque; uppercase + tracking for the brand line only.
BodyInter 400/500/600 at 13.5–14px. Line-height 1.5.
IdentifiersRoboto Mono for everything the government assigns: solicitation #s, NAICS, CGAC, notice ids. This platform is full of them — mono makes them scannable and copy-safe.
The facet barThe one piece of ornament: a 3px four-color rule (Brand Green → facet tints → Blue, the logo's geometry flattened) under the masthead. It is the family signature — use once per page, never inside content.
ElevationHairline borders + 0 1px 2px rgba(6,29,82,.06). One level. Data platforms don't float.
Radius10px cards, 6px controls, 999px pills.

Do

  • Reserve color for meaning: green = affirmative/active, blue = informational/navigational, amber = attention, ink = brand.
  • Keep evidence styling (match snippets, highlights) consistent between search results and watchlist hits — same component, same look.
  • State coverage and staleness in the shell (status line) on every corpus-backed surface.
  • Use the compass mark at small sizes; the full wordmark only in the masthead.

Don't

  • Don't use Brand Green #16A34A or the facet tints for text on white — they fail contrast; that's what Action Green is for.
  • Don't put the facet bar on cards, buttons, or tables — one per page, on the shell.
  • Don't bleed this shell into Meridian deliverables or vice versa; the client-facing brand stays navy/gold serif.
  • Don't introduce new hues per feature; a new state gets a tone from this table or a written amendment here.

Recommendation & mechanics

Recommendation: Option B. The platform joins ProcessAI under one recognizable family (relevant the moment profiles are sold or demoed), the mono-for-identifiers rule does real usability work in a government-ID-saturated UI, and the cost over Option A is near zero because both are a token swap behind src/components/ui. Adoption order: tokens in globals.css → watchlist UI ships on them → existing screens migrate as they're touched. Approve a direction and the watchlist UI debuts on it.
